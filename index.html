ABOUT US

AstroNova

Created and run by amateur astronomers, Spacenova aspires to provide a holistic offering that any astronomer would love. Stare at the skies through the telescopes in our observatories. Take a trip to the constellations with our astronomy tours. Learn about advances in astrophysics and space exploration through our workshops and online discussions with experts. See the universe differently through your camera lens at our astrophotography sessions. In this journey, all you need to bring along, is curiosity. Spacenova will be your uplink to the stars, your platform to the skies. Your gateway to the Universe.
